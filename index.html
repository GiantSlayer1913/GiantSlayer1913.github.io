About Me


My name is Mike and I'm a full stack web developer with a background in marketing and sales. In 2017, I came back home to Boston, MA after living in Tel Aviv, Israel for 3 years. I gained a lot of experience working in a variety of sales and marketing roles in startups to medium sized firms. Since graduating from a programming bootcamp (General Assembly) in June, 2018, I've been freelancing my skills updating old websites to creating new ones from scratch! Feel free to contact me via my details below. Prices are negotiable! In the meantime you can view my gallery of completed work located in the portfolio section.
