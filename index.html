About Me


My name is Mike and I'm a full stack web developer from the Greater Boston Area with a passion for Ruby/Rails and frontend frameworks. Recently, I graduated General Assembly's WDI bootcamp (June 2018) and have been working on new projects, honing my skills in frontend frameworks, and exploring new langauges like React and Meteor. Before GA's bootcamp, I lived in Tel Aviv, Israel for 3 years and worked in marketing and sales related roles. With new skills in web development, I plan on leveraging my background to make applications more user friendly and visually appealing. You can contact me via my details (email preferred) and view my gallery of completed work in the Projects section.
