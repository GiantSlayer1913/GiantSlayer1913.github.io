About Me




My name is Mike and I'm a full stack web developer with a background in sales, marketing and international work experience. In 2017, I came back to Boston after living in Israel for 3 years working in sales and marketing related roles. I wanted to gain some new skills in computer programming and after enrolling in a basic 4 week JavaScript course, I thought web development might be a good fit. I later enrolled in the WD Immersive program at General Assembly and graduated from the program in June, 2018. This portfolio is sepcifically designed to highlight my various skills and completed projects in full stack web development. Overall, I'm really excited to bring my Ruby/Rails skills and grounded perspective into the marketplace of web development!
